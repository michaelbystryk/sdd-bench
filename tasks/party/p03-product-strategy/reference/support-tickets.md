# Support tickets — sampling, last quarter

Pulled a representative slice from the queue (~30 of ~340 tickets). Tagged by the CS
lead with a rough theme. Ticket volume in parens after the theme is the *full-quarter*
count for that theme, not the sample count.

---

**[T-2231]** theme: ERP / reconciliation (98 this quarter)
"After we approve invoices in Lumen we still have to re-key everything into NetSuite,
or export a CSV and clean it up because the GL codes don't map. For 400 invoices a
month this is most of a full day of someone's week. Is there really no NetSuite sync?"
— Controller, 220-person logistics co.

**[T-2240]** theme: ERP / reconciliation
"We close the books on the 5th. The bottleneck is matching what we approved in Lumen
against what actually hit Sage. We do it in a spreadsheet. It's the single worst part
of our month." — AP Manager, 310-person SaaS co.

**[T-2255]** theme: ERP / reconciliation
"Does Lumen integrate with Intacct? Sales said 'CSV export.' That is not an
integration." — Controller.

**[T-2261]** theme: approval routing (61 this quarter)
"Love the approval routing but I want conditional rules — invoices over $10k should
need a second approver, and anything from [vendor] should auto-route to facilities.
Right now I rebuild the routing by hand each time someone joins or leaves." — AP Lead.

**[T-2266]** theme: approval routing
"The routing is the reason we picked you over [competitor]. Can it do approval *limits*
per person? Our auditors asked." — Controller, 180-person firm.

**[T-2270]** theme: approval routing
"Slack approvals are great. Half my approvers never log into Lumen, they just approve
from Slack. Please don't ever break that." — AP Manager.

**[T-2281]** theme: OCR accuracy (44 this quarter)

We fix it by hand. Annoying but livable." — AP clerk.

**[T-2288]** theme: OCR accuracy

— Controller.
