**Sales-leadership annotation (VP Sales):**

"Two clusters in here. The **enterprise cluster** (Atlas, Meridian) is the loudest in our
pipeline meetings — big logos, big projected ARR, lots of requirements (SSO, portal, SOC
2, SCIM). But: neither has a finance champion, both are procurement-led, both have been
stalled for 2+ quarters, and the projected ARR is AE optimism, not signed. We'd be
building an enterprise feature set (portal, SCIM, SOC 2) for two maybes who'd then want a
custom everything-else.

The **mid-market cluster** (Tindall, Dovetail, Pinebrook, Calderon, plus the Bellweather
expansion) is quieter per-deal but it's our actual ICP, the deals are finance-led with
champions, and the asks converge: **ERP sync** and **deeper routing**. Five deals, ~$160k
combined, all winnable this half, all asking for variants of the same two things.

My honest read: the enterprise deals suck up the most airtime in pipeline review and the
least likely to close. The mid-market asks are boring and they're where the money is."
